About the Project

OLDSALMO investigates the long-term effects of climate change and overfishing on Atlantic salmon using palaeogenomics, epigenetics, and environmental DNA.

Methodology

We use high-throughput sequencing to analyze ancient, historical, and contemporary salmon genomes to track genetic erosion and adaptation over the past 16,000 years.
